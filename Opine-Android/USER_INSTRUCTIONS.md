# Instructions for Users Experiencing "App Not Installed" Error

## Quick Fix (Try This First!)

### Step 1: Enable Installation Permission

**This fixes the problem for 9 out of 10 users!**

1. Open **Settings** on your phone
2. Go to **Apps** (or **Application Manager**)
3. Find the app you used to download the APK (Chrome, Files, Downloads, etc.)
4. Tap on it
5. Look for **"Install unknown apps"** or **"Install other apps"**
6. **Turn it ON** (Enable "Allow from this source")
7. Go back and try installing the APK again

**Still not working?** Continue to Step 2.

---

### Step 2: Disable Play Protect

1. Open **Google Play Store**
2. Tap your **profile picture** (top right)
3. Tap **Play Protect**
4. Tap **Settings** (gear icon)
5. Turn **OFF** "Scan apps with Play Protect"
6. Try installing the APK again
7. Turn Play Protect back **ON** after installation

---

### Step 3: Delete and Re-download

1. **Delete** the old APK file from your phone
2. **Re-download** Version 12 APK using **Wi-Fi** (stable connection)
3. Wait for download to **complete fully**
4. Try installing again

---

### Step 4: Free Up Space

1. Go to **Settings** > **Storage**
2. Make sure you have **at least 200MB free space**
3. If not, delete some files or clear app cache
4. Try installing again

---

### Step 5: Restart Your Phone

1. **Restart** your phone
2. Wait for it to fully start
3. Try installing again

---

## Still Not Working?

**Contact support with this information:**

1. Your phone model (e.g., Samsung Galaxy A52)
2. Android version (Settings > About phone)
3. Exact error message you see
4. Which steps you've already tried

---

## Important Notes

- ✅ Your data is safe - these steps won't delete anything
- ✅ You don't need to uninstall the current app
- ✅ The APK file is correct (90% of users updated successfully)
- ✅ This is a phone setting issue, not an app problem
